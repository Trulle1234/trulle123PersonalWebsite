About Me

I’m a 14-year-old from Sweden who enjoys programming, photography, and filmmaking. I’m an active member of Hack Club. Right now, I’m learning web development — this website is one of my learning projects.

My interest in programming started at a young age when my dad introduced me to Scratch. After a while, I thought Scratch was too easy, so I decided to learn something new — Python. Using a course by "Unga Programmerare," I learned the basics and later discovered Pygame Zero. I stayed at that level for a while, jumping around and trying new things. But about 2 months ago, a Hack Club hackathon (Shipwrecked) gave me the motivation I needed, and now I’m making this website as my final project to earn my invite.
